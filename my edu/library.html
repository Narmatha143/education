Library is the heart, mind and soul of an educational institution and a fountain-head of innovativeness, inspiration and vision both for the students and the faculty. Learning and library are supportive to each other as the light to the lamp. Library at T.J.S. Engineering College offers a fully integrated and dynamic environment for conducting academic study.

Library Facilities at T.J.S have noticeable features

  1. Digital Library
  2. Member of DELNET
  3. NPTEL facility (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning) to access Video lectures
  4. Subscribing a large number of magazines and dailies
  5. Wide Collection of National and International Journals
  6. Fully computerized system for issuing, search and returning of the books
  7. Reference Service is given to assist right information to the user at right time
  8. Reading facility for 100 users
  9. Collection of Thesis and Dissertation for consultation of users
